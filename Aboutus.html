Azerbaijan State Oil and Industry University

At present, the Azerbaijan State Oil and Industry University (ADNSU) trains specialists and staff on the basis of the Constitution of the Republic of Azerbaijan, the Law of the Republic of Azerbaijan "On Education", orders and decisions of the Cabinet of Ministers and the Ministry of Education, Education Reform Program and other normative legal documents.ADNSU provides training at all three levels of higher education - bachelor's, master's and doctoral. At present, the university has 7 faculties and 27 departments. Up to 13,000 undergraduate students in various scientific fields, including 333 students in SABAH groups and 410 students in UFAZ. 1180 students continue their education at the master's level, and 244 people are engaged in scientific activity at the doctoral level.
